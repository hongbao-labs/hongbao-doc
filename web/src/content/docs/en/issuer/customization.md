**Most of a Hongbao card face is open canvas** — the vast majority of it is yours to brand. Only a small set of required elements stay reserved, so cardholders can recognize a trusted Hongbao card and claim without friction.

> This specification is under active development. Fields marked `[TBD]` are not yet finalized; the latest signed commercial agreement takes precedence. For quotes or design templates, contact us via [Contact](../contact).

| Location | Content | Reason |
